About us

Luxuries was started as a small corner shop; the simple idea of selling more for less has grown over the last 65 years into the biggest retail chain. Each year, Needs and luxuries serves millions of customers nationwide, 9 branches in Karachi, Sindh & 2 branches in Faisalabad & Gujranwala, Punjab, Pakistan. We have more than 6000+ employees nationwide. Needs and Luxuries continues to be the leader in sustainability, corporate philanthropy and employment opportunity. It’s all part of our unwavering commitment to creating opportunities and bringing the value to customers and communities across Pakistan. Since the first small corner shop opened in 1955 by M. Hakim Khan Abbasi at Bahadurabad, Karachi. We dedicated our legacy for making a difference in the lives & the shopping experience of our customers. Our successful business story is the result of XYZ visionary leadership, along with the generations of associates; this rich heritage defines who we are and what we do. We are more focused on helping customers and communities to save money and enjoy the ultimate shopping experience everyday.
